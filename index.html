ClockClock 24

A kinetic clock installation, re-interprets and re-shapes the concept of time with creativity, technology, and artistic breath.

Kinetic

The individual clock hands veer from the unpredictable, mechanical spinning to perfect synchronization, which illustrating a shifting choreography of disarray and structure.

Tangible

Handcrafted material makes sense of quality, reality and electronic. Brief style of flat color combination performs the synchronic ultimate aesthetics.

Smart

Cross-platform software helps to remote control the device. Multiple modes are easily switched to. Functional setting are provided, such as time synchronization, fine tune calibration and etc.
